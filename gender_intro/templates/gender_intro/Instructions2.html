Roles: Decider or Receiver

  • In this experiment, there are two possible roles: Decider or Receiver.
  • Your role will be determined randomly at the beginning of the experiment.
  • You will keep this same role for the entire experiment.
  • You will learn your role before making any decisions.

Rounds & Matching:

  • There will be {{ Constants.number_rounds }} rounds in this experiment.
  • In every round, each Decider will be matched with a new Receiver.
  • You will never be matched with the same player for more than a single round over the course of the experiment.

The Decider's Choice:

  • In each of the {{ Constants.num_rounds }} rounds, the Receiver is given an endowment of {{ Constants.endowment }}.
  • The Decider will then have the opportunity to take any part of the Receiver's {{ Constants.endowment }} endowment.

The Receiver's earnings:

  • After the Decider chooses how much to take from the Receiver's {{ Constants.endowment }} endowment, the Receiver is left with whatever remains.
    • So if the Decider chooses to take $X of the Receiver's {{ Constants.endowment }}, then in that round the Decider gets $X while the Receiver gets {{ Constants.endowment }} - $X.
