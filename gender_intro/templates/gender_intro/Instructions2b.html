The Decider's Choice:

  • In each of the {{ Constants.number_rounds }} rounds, the Receiver is given an endowment of {{ Constants.endowment }}.
  • The Decider will then have the opportunity to take any part of the Receiver's {{ Constants.endowment }} endowment.

The Payoff Round:

  • Of the {{ Constants.number_rounds }} rounds, one round will be chosen randomly for payment.

The Receiver's Earnings:

  • After the Decider chooses how much to take from the Receiver's {{ Constants.endowment }} endowment, the Receiver is left with whatever money remains.
    • So if the Decider chooses to take $X of the Receiver's {{ Constants.endowment }}, and if that round is chosen for payment, then the Receiver keeps {{ Constants.endowment }} - $X and the Decider gets $X.
